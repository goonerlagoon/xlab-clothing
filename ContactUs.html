FAQ?

Frequently Asked Questions? Answer!
What time is best to contact you? We are open from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM EST every weekday, where you can contact us through email at labclothing@support.com or call us at (888) 888-8888.
Who do I contact if I am unable to login? Please shoot our technical support team a email at xlabclothing@support.com
How long does it usually take for me to receive my clothing? Usually it would take 5-7 business days unless you choose expedited shipping.
Please note expedited will make your shipping time much faster and will cost more. Please choose shipping at checkout.
Is there a brick and mortar store? Or is it only online? Our offices and warehouses are based in New York... but we unfortuanately do not have a brick and mortar just online at the moment. Sorry!
What is the return window? We only do exchanges as returns are not allowed. Thank you!
When do you normally drop your new clothing lines? We usually like to follow the fashion industry and drop our lines on their timeline such as Fall, Spring, Summer, etc.
Your clothing is organic? Our organic clothing is made from natural fibres, which are derived from plants. An important factor to note about our plants are that they do not grow through the help of pesticides, herbicides, synthetic fertilisers and no genetically modified organisms (GMOs).
